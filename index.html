About

Greetings from Emmanuel Church! Whether you’re new to Philadelphia or a longtime local, we would love to have you join us for worship!
We are located at 4723 Spruce Street, Philadelphia, PA 19139,
just a couple of blocks west of University City.

Emmanuel is part of the Presbyterian Church in America (PCA):

1. Faithful to the Scriptures
2. True to the Reformed Faith
3. Obedient to the Great Commission

To learn more about our denomination, please visit: www.pcanet.org

Our Vision

We, Emmanuel Church, aim to be a missional church that strives to obey and fulfill the Lord’s Great Commission, from Philadelphia to the ends of the Earth

Our Mission

To make, mature, and multiply disciples of Jesus Christ by sharing the gospel, shepherding God’s people, and serving with our gifts.

Our Values

Our vision statement is supported by five core values:

1. Worship – glorifying and enjoying God forever
2. Discipleship – making followers of Jesus Christ
3. Fellowship – building one another up in Christ
4. Serving – edifying the church through our gifts
5. Witnessing – spreading the gospel to Philadelphia

Sunday Worship

Our worship service begins at 9:45 am. We meet in the 3rd Floor Sanctuary of our building. Please look for a member of the Hospitality Team in the lobby!

Friday Night Fellowship

Every Friday, from 8:00 to 10:00 pm, we gather together for Bible Study. We’d love to have you join us for this fellowship! We also have a dinner followed by a praise and prayer night on the 1st Friday of every month.

Small Groups

We also meet regularly in different small groups to study the Bible and pray with one another. Please let us know if you’d like to get plugged into one of our small groups!
